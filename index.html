About The Product

Why we built Modulus.

This project was inspired by my own struggles with sleep quality and the impact it can have on daily performance, focus, and overall well-being. Despite getting enough hours of sleep, I often found myself waking up groggy and unable to consistently perform at my best throughout the day. That experience led me to explore how technology, specifically EEG-based brain activity monitoring, could provide deeper insight into sleep cycles and recovery patterns. My vision for this project is to create a wearable solution that helps people better understand and optimize their sleep, enabling them to improve their energy, productivity, and long-term health.
